Avatar

The story follows Jake Sully (played by Sam Worthington), a paraplegic former Marine who is recruited to participate in the Avatar Program after the death of his twin brother. The program allows humans to remotely control bioengineered hybrid bodies, known as avatars, which are a mix of human and Na'vi DNA. The Na'vi are the indigenous people of Pandora, a humanoid species that lives in harmony with nature
